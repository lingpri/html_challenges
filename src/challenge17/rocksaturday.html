Homecoming at high school is an exciting and cherished annual tradition that brings together students, alumni, and the community in a celebration of school spirit and unity. Amidst the lively festivities and electrifying football game, volunteer opportunities offer a unique and rewarding way for students to give back to their community. One noteworthy initiative is the volunteering at the railway museum, today akshara had the oppurtunity to engage in educational activities and preserve the rich history of the railway system. This opportunity not only instills a sense of pride and responsibility within the students but also allows them to showcase their dedication and professionalism to their peers. By contributing their time and skills, students actively contribute to the success and vibrancy of the homecoming experience, creating cherished memories that will last a lifetime.
